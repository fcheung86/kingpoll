How does this work?

Coke or Pepsi? Cat or dog? Do aliens exist? Fold or crumple? Want to know if you're part of an elite minority with special preferences? You're in the right place.

You see a poll, you vote. Don't see a poll? Make one. Don't want people knowing what you've voted? Vote anonymously. Only show what you want to show.

Anonymous?

Yeah. ANONYMOUS. Signing up is annoying; I don't want to ruin your fun. Just vote; your votes are cool. But we'll be adding special features for accounts in the future so sign up to reserve your username!

How do you make sure people can't vote more than once?

We use a number of basic and weird analytical gimmicks to keep track of your votes. It's not 100% but hopefully we've identified each user well enough to get 90%+ accuracy! We're just going to have to wait and see as the numbers come in.

What can I do on this site?

Let's start with why I made this site: I'm a curious person. Humans are curious by nature. Questions -- sometimes trivial, sometimes hypothetical -- would arise within my social circle and arguments over what's better, would surely follow.

What if you could quickly and easily ask the world a crazy question and get an honest answer? Mindblasting. So how would you do this? Facebook? You can't just randomly add people and ask creepy questions. OK, so Twitter? You have like 9 followers, don't kid yourself. OK, why don't I just go to one of the million other survey sites out there? You can, but they care only about themselves. They make you sign up, pay for their services, or they give you a simple and free poll link, but you need to get it out there yourself. It's scary out there, and sometimes you don't want people knowing what you're thinking, but you so desperately want to know what others think. Oh man, that's deep.

Then comes along this site. Ask what you want; let your thoughts run wild and free. You're behind a veil of anonymity; free from judgement, free from inconveniences, just free as you can be.

What is the end goal of this site?

We want to be the Wikipedia of opinions. You can google your questions but depending on where you go, biases will always skew the answers. If we can get everyone using our site, we can get an accurate opinion on any question in a centralized place that anyone can access and contribute.

And some time in the future, when the world trusts us enough, we hope to implement a voting system for elections around the world. But this will be in the very FAR future. What we want know is for everyone to just have fun using our platform.
